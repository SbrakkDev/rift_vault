RuneShelf

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what data RuneShelf uses, why it is used, and how it supports collection, deck builder, companion, friends, and community features.

Last updated: March 27, 2026

What RuneShelf stores

RuneShelf may store the following information when you use the app:

  • Account information such as your email address and username.
  • Your card collection, favorites, and custom card lists.
  • Your decks, deck versions, and match history.
  • Friend relationships and public community deck data you choose to share.
  • Basic technical information required for authentication and sync.

Why this data is used

RuneShelf uses this information only to provide app functionality, including:

  • sign in and secure access to your account;
  • sync your data across devices;
  • save decks, favorites, lists, and collection progress;
  • enable optional social features such as friends, public decks, likes, and views;
  • show match history and companion-related data.

Data sharing

RuneShelf does not sell personal data. Information is only shared when required to operate the service or when you intentionally make content public, such as a public deck or public match history.

Third-party services

RuneShelf may rely on third-party services for infrastructure, such as:

  • authentication and cloud storage;
  • email delivery for sign-in codes;
  • community data sync and app hosting.

These services are used only to support the operation of the app.

Public content

If you publish a deck or enable public match history, that content may be visible to other RuneShelf users. Favorites and other collection data remain private unless explicitly exposed through a public feature.

Your choices

You can control or remove app content by editing or deleting it inside RuneShelf. You may also request account-related support by contacting the developer.
