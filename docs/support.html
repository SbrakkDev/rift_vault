Support

Need help with RuneShelf? This page includes contact details and basic support information for users and for App Review.

Contact support

For questions, bug reports, account issues, or support requests, contact RuneShelf support by email.

Support email

runeshelfapp@gmail.com

How to request help

  • Describe the issue or question clearly.
  • Include your device model and iOS version when relevant.
  • Mention the app area involved, such as Binder, Deck Builder, Companion, Community, or Friends.
  • Add screenshots if they help explain the problem.

Privacy and account requests

If your request concerns account access, personal data, or account deletion, please specify that in your email so it can be handled as a privacy-related support request.
